How this is calculated:

Revenue: The total money collected from customer bills today.
Cost of Goods Sold (COGS): The original Buy Price of the exact items you sold today.
Gross Profit: Revenue minus COGS. (e.g., Sold a pen for ₹10 that you bought for ₹6 = ₹4 Profit).
Note: Stock purchased from suppliers today goes into inventory value, it does not deduct directly from today's profit until the item is actually sold.
